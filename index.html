The Fabric of Luxury

Experience the
Material Difference

Hover to feel each texture. Each fabric is chosen for its unique sensory quality and enduring beauty.

Iris Collection

Velvet

An opulent woven fabric with a distinctive soft pile, velvet catches light to create a luminous, ever-changing depth of color. Sumptuous warmth meets enduring elegance.

Nadia Collection

Woven Jacquard

Intricate patterns woven directly into the fabric during creation — not printed on. Each design is architectural in its construction, with raised texture you can feel as well as see.

Tilly & Ava

Cotton Percale Blend

A crisp, cool weave with a matte finish. Percale becomes softer with every wash — a fabric that improves with time, like the finest linen shirts.

Iris Collection

Tessuto

Pres Les Tessuto sheeting — our proprietary woven fabric with a subtle sheen and buttery hand-feel. Developed exclusively for the Iris collection as the perfect velvet companion.
